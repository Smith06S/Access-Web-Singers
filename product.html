Technical Terms

Action
The mechanical parts of a piano that transfer the motion from the keys to the hammers that strike the strings.
Soundboard
A large wooden board that amplifies the vibrations of the strings to produce sound.
Hammers
Felt-covered mechanisms that strike the strings to produce sound when a key is pressed.
Dampers
Devices that stop the strings from vibrating when a key is released, ending the note.
